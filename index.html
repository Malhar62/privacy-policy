Data Collection

We collect minimal personal information to enhance your app experience:

  • Email and Name: You may optionally provide your name and email for your profile within the app.
  • No authentication required: No authentication or login is required to use the app.
  • No sensitive data or permissions: We do not collect any sensitive personal data or access device permissions like contacts, location, camera, or storage.

Data Usage

Your profile details are used only within the app:

  • Your email and name are used only to personalize your profile.
  • Expense-related data (amount, category, notes, etc.) is stored locally on your device.
  • The app does not share, transmit, or upload any information to external servers.

Data Storage & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.

  • We do not store any data on remote servers or cloud storage.
  • When the app is uninstalled, all stored data — including expenses and profile details — is permanently deleted.

Internet Usage

The app functions fully offline and does not require internet access.

  • No data is transmitted over the internet.
  • No analytics, advertisements, or trackers are used in the app.

Language Support

The app is available in the following languages:

  • English
  • Hindi

You can switch the language from within the app settings.
